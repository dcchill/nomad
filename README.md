<p style="text-align: center;">Nomad is a storage and gadget focused addon for Create that adds balanced and useful tools to make your life easier.  These items are designed to integrate into Create's original aesthetic and balance.</p>

## Features

*   Dyeable and Curios-slot compatible **Backpacks** that can automatically refill stacks in your hotbar.
*   The **Trackpack**, which holds a massive ammount of Tracks and also refills your hotbar.
*   A **Grappling Hook** that uses backtank air to let you quickly traverese the world.
*   The **Portable Mechanical Saw** uses backtank air to chop down trees in the same way the standard Mechanical Saw would.
*   Lastly, the **Jackhammer**, which functions like a pickaxe but mines entire veins of ores at once.  Also uses backtank air.
*   And more to come!
